About Us

Learning never stops at Vaishri

Welcome to vaishri, your number one source for educating a student. We are dedicated to providing you the very best learning experience with an emphasis on value-based learning.

Who are we

We are a group of enthusiasts (certified graduates as well) who took initiative in establishing an educational academy having in mind all the difficulties we faced during our education. No more we want the same thing to be experienced by the students. So, we have come up with the best practices to make the learning process interesting and engaging and also to help students to get good grades in their Schooling which indeed leads to a strong foundation in the career as well.

What we do

We ensure that we are filling the gap between the regular lectures and conceptual understanding of students, through which you can see a considerable change in the academics of a child which is a byproduct of learning the things in a way that they need to be. We also ensure the students are assessed with worksheets on daily basis to make sure that they are engaged with the classes. We focus on maximizing student’s potential and dedication. We also provide you the best educational videos which help you to understand the difficult topic clearly. We train our students with the extra ability to handle questionnaires in a logical way. In short, we provide you a learning platform to enhance your knowledge in your field of academics in a smart, comfortable, and interesting way.

What you gain

As a parent, you can be confident about your child’s academic status as we also provide an assessment report of your child’s performance and make progression in it. As a student, you will be exposed to an interactive way of learning and easy ways to understand and memorize the concepts.
