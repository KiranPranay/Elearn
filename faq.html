“I always lose interest in my studies during my preparation” - Any student
This happens because they are not taught in a way that makes them feel subject to be interesting and engaging. But we at Vaishri make sure that even the worksheets which are given to complete are student-centric unlike creating a workload on students.

Most of the students feel the learning process to be tedious which indeed is not the reality. The whole fault lies in the ways we adapt to learn things. We at Vaishri ensure that the child is able to understand the core concept and is able to reflect on them properly with the help of daily assessments. We also provide one-on-one mentorship depending on the student’s performance to make sure that they are getting benefited from the course curriculum, unlike the normal ones which are designed the same for all the students.

Vaishri itself is specialized in value-based learning which will ensure that student also gains insights about what all things he/she needs to prioritize apart from their academics. Our lectures do not just contain the content given in the textbook rather we will also relate and reflect on daily life examples which will make sure that the child is able to look at the things practically.
